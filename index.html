Artemis

getartemis.xyz • 2021–2022

This is a tribute to the original Artemis Reborn app store, a quiet rebel in a world of revokes and ads. No shady popups. No bloated nonsense. Just clean delivery of what users wanted—apps, jailbreaks, and the moonlight aesthetic to go with it.

Artemis may be gone, but it mattered. This site is a digital shrine to honour its existence and the hands that built it.
